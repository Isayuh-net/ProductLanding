Emporio Armani Stronger With You Powerfully



A bold, modern take on the iconic Stronger With You line — powerful but approachable with a killer presence.

  • A confident masculine scent that announces itself without overwhelming. Longevity is solid, often lasting 7–9 hours, with lingering skin scent after that.
  • Projection can be noticeable — perfect for nights out or special moments.
  • Bold & attention-grabbing
  • Youthful yet refined
  • Great for evening or cooler months

Valentino Born in Roma Intense

A sensual and magnetic fragrance that blends classic Italian luxury with modern depth

  • A warm, amber-vanilla powerhouse with sophisticated layers that give it intensity and character.
  • Think rich vanilla with floral brightness and a smokier woody base — elegant yet seductive.
  • Bold but polished
  • Romantic and memorable
  • Ideal for date nights or evening affairs

Creed Aventus Absolu

A refined evolution of the iconic Aventus, designed for those who want confidence, complexity, and performance.

  • Lasts around 8–10 hours with noticeable projection initially.
  • Complex and layered with a bold, masculine profile.
  • Features a powerful blend of citrus, spices, and woody notes.
  • Long-lasting with excellent projection and sillage.
  • Perfect for confident individuals who appreciate luxury and sophistication.
